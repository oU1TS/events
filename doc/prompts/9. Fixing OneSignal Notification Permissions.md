### How to get your `ONESIGNAL_APP_ID`

The App ID in the codebase (`cff59aa0-39c1-4d35-9560-b23409f6d014`) is a **placeholder App ID**. To connect your live site to OneSignal:

1. **Sign in to OneSignal**: Go to [dashboard.onesignal.com](https://dashboard.onesignal.com/).
2. **Select or Create an App**: Click **New App/Website** (or choose your existing app), and select **Web Push**.
3. **Copy your App ID & API Key**:
   - In OneSignal Dashboard, navigate to **Settings** $\rightarrow$ **Keys & IDs**.
   - Copy your **OneSignal App ID** (a UUID string like `xxxxxxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxxxxxxxxxx`).
   - Copy your **REST API Key** (starts with `os_v2_...` or similar).
4. **Update your project**:
   - Replace `"cff59aa0-39c1-4d35-9560-b23409f6d014"` in [index.html](file:///c:/Users/.../Documents/GitHub/%5BoU1TS%5D/events/index.html#L26) with your real **App ID**.
   - In your GitHub Repository, go to **Settings** $\rightarrow$ **Secrets and variables** $\rightarrow$ **Actions**, and add:
     - `ONESIGNAL_APP_ID` (your App ID)
     - `ONESIGNAL_REST_API_KEY` (your REST API Key)
